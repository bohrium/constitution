                            O R D E R

     Upon consideration of the respondents' petition for rehearing en banc, the
response thereto, and the vote by a majority of the judges of the court in regular, active
service in favor of the petition, it is

     ORDERED that the petition be granted.  This case will be reheard by the court
sitting en banc.  The judgment filed on February 8, 2002, is hereby vacated.  It is

     FURTHER ORDERED that an order governing further proceedings will issue
separately.
