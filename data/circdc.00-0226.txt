     BEFORE:   Edwards, Chief Judge and Rogers, Circuit Judge

                            O R D E R

     Upon consideration of petitioners' motion to recall mandate or for an order
pursuant to 47 U.S.C.  402(h) or a writ of mandamus to compel agency action (filed on
July 6, 2000), the supplement and responses thereto, petitioners' emergency motion
and supplement to the motion to recall mandate or for an order pursuant to 47 U.S.C. 
402(h) or a writ of mandamus to compel agency action, (filed on October 2, 2000), the
responses and reply thereto, it is

     ORDERED that the motion to recall the mandate be granted and the petition for
a writ of mandamus to the Federal Communications Commission be granted for the
reasons set forth in the opinion issued this date.  

     The Clerk is directed to reissue the mandate to the Commission forthwith.
